Tip 1 : Resale

  • By yourself

    • Many websites like Ebay, Le Bon Coin or Vinted allow you to sell your clothes online, choosing your prices.


  • Through a third party

    • Opt for the deposit-sale. The concept: we put his clothes in a thrift store that sells them for us and gives us some of the money collected.

Tip 2 : Give

Why not give clothes that we no longer wear instead of letting them mope at the bottom of our closets ? You can give them to your friends, family, or to associations that badly need it like Emmaüs, La Croix Rouge or Le Secours Populaire. You can also moving towards re-use structures such as recycling.

Tip 3 : Barter

You gather in someone's house without forgetting to bring all the clothes that are no longer worn and we come back with those whose loved ones no longer want. Ideally, each one presents a garment still in good condition that he or she wishes to give and the one who covets it is manifested. If several people are interested, each one tries it and the one to whom the garment goes best wins (one can also decide on other rules!). So on until all the clothes have been exchanged.
